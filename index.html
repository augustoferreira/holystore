    How It Works

    Just hold the power button once to turn it on, and press the same button to cycle through different colors and lighting modes until you find the perfect vibe.

    Once installed, the built-in induction sensor does the rest — the light automatically turns on when you open your car door and shuts off when you close it, saving power without you lifting a finger. It won’t turn on when exposed to strong sunlight, making it even more efficient. It’s simple, smart, and made to glow when you do.
